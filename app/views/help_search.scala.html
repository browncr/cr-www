Searching the Critical Review

Enter your search criteria in the boxes on the left side of every page.

Course Code

You can enter course codes in any format:

  • GEOL0220
  • CSCI0310
  • CS31
  • MATH054

You can also enter a comma-separated list of departments, such as: HIST, CS, POLS.

Departments

If you want to limit your search to certain departments, check them off in this box.

Recently Reviewed

If you check this box, only courses which have been reviewed in the last four years will be displayed.
